Headline: Manchester City exit provides Chelsea with clear £54m incentive
Summary: Manchester City was eliminated from the FIFA Club World Cup at the last-16 stage, bringing an end to their participation in the tournament. A Chelsea victory would significantly aid their financial position for the new year and embolden them to pursue a transfer strategy aimed at becoming Premier League title challengers.
